About


Hello, my name is Anunay Mahajan I'm an Experience Designer, passionate about constructing gainful user-centric experiences.

Being a design enthusiast I always strive for an opportunity to enhance user's experiences of using products and services. I love the process of design thinking for solving complex design hitches and glitches. I see potential in challenges, follow design approach to solve problems and take feedback as an opportunity to create value. Practicing this approach in day-to-day life has been critical to my success at deriving customer insights and pursuing innovations. I love working in dynamic environments, practicing a broad set of design skills, and building connections in the design community.

While I'm not at work, I love doing photography, doodling and gaming. I hold my special interest in Martial Arts, Sustainability and Augmented Reality.

Thanks for stopping by, I'm always interested in hearing from creative people so feel free to contact me on anunay.mahajan@gmail.com or connect on my following social accounts.
