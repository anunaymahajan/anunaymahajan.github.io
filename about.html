Hello.



I'm Anunay Mahajan, Crafting transformative e-Cockpit solutions at Tata Motors Design Studio, India.

I specialize in integrating automotive branding with cutting-edge HMI and UX design to create seamless, impactful experiences. My work spans the entire design journey—from concept through to launch— ensuring every digital and physical interaction strengthens brand identity and resonates deeply with users.

A key focus is the seamless integration of vehicle branding into every touchpoint, making each interaction not only functional but emotionally engaging. This holistic approach ensures the vehicle’s personality is consistently reflected through unified visual and experiential elements.

Driven by consumer insights and design thinking, I craft intuitive, human-centered solutions that stand out. My expertise in Multi-Sensorial User Experience explores how sensory elements—sight, sound, and touch—enhance brand impact, while Mixed Reality technologies redefine physical-digital interactions. Sustainability remains a core principle in all design processes.

Passionate about creating experiences that drive brand loyalty, I’m eager to connect with professionals to explore innovative collaborations and push the boundaries of automotive design. Let’s innovate together!
