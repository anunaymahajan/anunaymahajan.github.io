About



Hi! I'm Anunay, an Experience Designer passionate about crafting gainful user-centric experiences.

Here is my story


Design is always my thing since high school, where I made posters, presentations, web pages for various events. That’s when I knew I like designing for people.

I studied Electronics and Communication engineering, as I wasn’t aware of any design education programme. Though engineering wasn’t my choice, but it gave me a strong background in research, communication and knowledge of the technology. Also, it provided me exposure to various commination medias, where I understood the importance of communication in human life. I always had a strong a desire to be a problem solver and a designer. The more exposure to design world I got, the deeper I started diving into it. I was driven to design because I felt excited and rewarding to learn about people’s goals and desires, help them solve problems and make their lives easier.

So, post-graduation, instead of engineering I choose Design as a career option and successfully landed as User Experience Designer. And now, I’m continuously learning design form several online and offline sources, and practicing my learnings to nourish my creative skills

Being a design enthusiast, I always strive for an opportunity to enhance user's experiences of using products and services. I love applying the process of design thinking: I see potential in challenges, define design strategy to solve complex problems and take feedback as an opportunity to create value. Practicing this approach in day-to-day life has been critical to my success at deriving customer insights and pursuing innovations. I like practicing a broad set of design skills in dynamic environments and building connections in the design community.

While I'm not at work, I love doing photography, doodling and gaming. I hold my special interest in Martial Arts, Sustainability and Augmented Reality.

btw, thanks for stopping by and listening to my story
