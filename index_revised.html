ABOUT ME

Designer and developer from Dallas, Texas.
Currently based in Washington, DC.

I'm currently exploring how technology can better get information to readers and listeners of The Washington Post. I'm fascinated by all-things psychology and design, and love the idea of creating products that can change someone's life for the better.

I've been a mental health researcher, taught UX and web development to federal executives and college students, and a spoken word poet.
